Once upon a time in the QQZ world
There are beings known as particles
their world is 2d, if they go off the side of the world it wraps around to the other.
They try to dodge each other, but if they collide
there is a X% chance that they will mix their DNA and mutate.
When they mutate they become a mix of their own characteristics and the characteristics of the one they bumped into.
The characteristics of a particle is:
size: (1 - 8)
speed: (1 - 8)
color: (1 - 16)
direction: (1-8, representing n, ne, e, s, sw, w, nw)
